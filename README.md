## About

I'm a UX/UI Designer with 3+ years of experience crafting end-to-end digital products across web and mobile. I currently work at CentraLogic, designing customer-facing platforms for UK energy clients — from onboarding journeys to full customer portals. Before that, I spent two years at Capgemini building enterprise application interfaces for large-scale clients.

My work spans the full design process: research, wireframing, interaction design, high-fidelity UI, and handing off to engineering. I work primarily in Figma and care a lot about design systems and getting the details right in implementation.
