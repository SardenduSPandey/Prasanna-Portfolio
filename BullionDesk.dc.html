Context

Domain Context & Problem Discovery

Bullion Desk originated from direct exposure to real jewellery shop operations, where pricing decisions are made rapidly under constant market fluctuation. Jewellery pricing is not based on raw market rates alone. Every transaction involves adjusting for margin, purity, and sourcing conditions often handled mentally or through fragmented tools.

Strategic insight

"The gap was not in accessing market rates, but in translating them into actionable pricing."

The Core Friction

  • ✓Market rates vary across cities and sources
  • ✓Every jeweller applies a custom margin
  • ✓Final price depends on judgement, not system support
  • ✓Manual calculations increase cognitive load and errors
  • ✓No unified workflow for pricing

Jewellers don't operate on market rates. They operate on their own adjusted rates.

The turning point

Most tools focus only on market rates.

But jewellers make decisions based on their own adjusted shop rates.

The real unit of decision making is not the market rate, but a personalized shop adjusted rate.

Transactional pivot

From Market Rate to Shop Rate

Market rates are only a reference point. Jewellers adjust prices based on margin, sourcing, and local conditions.

● The problem

Manual adjustment leads to slower transactions, higher error risk, and reliance on experience.

● The solution

Introduce a Shop Rate system that converts market rate into shop rate, applies GST, and displays final selling price instantly.

Process comparison
BEFOREMarket Rate → Mental Calculation → Final Price
AFTERMarket Rate → Shop Rate → Ready to use
Impact metric

Eliminates manual calculations, reduces errors, and speeds up customer interactions at the counter.

"The system shifts pricing from mental effort to system-driven accuracy."

Transactional logic

Structured Exchange Workflow

Designed for use at the counter where speed and accuracy impact customer trust.

1. Select metal

Define the base asset class (Gold, Silver) for the exchange.

2. Enter weight

Input gross weight with precision handling for various unit measurements.

3. Choose purity

Select from preset purity chips or provide custom input for non standard items.

4. Apply deduction

Calculate wastage or stone weight deductions to reach net metal value.

System output values
EXCHANGE VALUEInstant Calculation
PURE GOLD WEIGHTBefore & After Deduction
EQUIVALENT VALUES22K / 18K Reference
Operational impact

Reduces complexity and improves transparency for both the jeweller and the customer.
